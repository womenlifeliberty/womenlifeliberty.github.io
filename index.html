Mahsa Zhina Amini

a 22-years-old Iranian woman, was just days from embarking on her undergraduate degree journey. Sadly, tragedy took her life as Mahsa died on September 16, 2022, due to injuries suffered while in custody of Iranian 'Morality Police' and being severely beaten for not wearing a proper hijab.
Mahsa's death has sparked protests across Iran and international communities. Iranian government has limited (and in many area completely shut down) internet access. This has made it almost impossible to provide a first-hand and impartial view into ongoing crackdowns of women and protesters.
